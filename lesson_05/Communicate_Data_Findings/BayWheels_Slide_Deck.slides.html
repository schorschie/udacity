Ford BeyWheels data findings¶

by Grzegorz Lippe¶

Investigation Overview¶

Lyft, formerly known as BayWheels shares its ride data on lyft.com.

Dataset Overview¶

The data is provided in monthly sets and was combined to one 2019 data set. It provides information for Driver ID, Start Point, End Point, the start and end time, and therefor also the ride duration.

Ride durations¶

  • One interesting matter is the duration of rides.
  • Do people use the service for shorter or longer trips?
  • How many trips did they book?

How far did the different bikes go?¶

Investigateing the bikes ride durations, travelled distances and average speeds showed that most of the bikes travel only 200 km or 35 hours before they are replaced. But there is a significant difference beween "early" ID bikes and later versions.
